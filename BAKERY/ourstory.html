OUR STORY

Bakery Store formed in 1977 when bakers from all Denver residence halls were consolidated into a single group.

We proudly produce hand-decorated products, made-from-scratch breads, bagels, cakes, and cookies, which are served in military campus. Our staff consists of skilled bakers with over 150 years of combined experience.
